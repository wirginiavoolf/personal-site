stephanie lane sutton is a midwestern poet, performer, and interactive media artist. Currently, she is a Michener Fellow in the University of Miami's Creative Writing MFA Program. Previously, she lived in Chicago, where she studied poetry at Columbia College Chicago and was a teaching artist with After School Matters. She was born in Detroit.

Sutton's poems have recently appeared in Day One, Arsenic Lobster, Moonsick Magazine, Tinderbox Poetry, THRUSH Poetry Journal, and Jai-Alai Magazine. As a member of the West Side School for the Desperate Collective, she created experimental performance and video work with co-member, Julia Victor. Sutton also creates text-based interactive media projects, such as the Gertrude Steinifier.

Sutton is a Co-Founding Editor of |tap| lit mag, an online literary magazine for marginalized writers, as well as a Poetry Editor of Sinking City.

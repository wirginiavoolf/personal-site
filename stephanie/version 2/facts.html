Facts About Distance




"Facts About Distance" is an interactive poem & mixed media art object. It was created using an Arduino microcomputer and C/C++ and includes an original poem by the author. It was showcased at Canes Film Fest on May 4th, 2017. "Facts About Distance" is dedicated to Desmond Crafton, a performance poet who passed away in February.

Facts About Distance
by Stephanie Lane Sutton

what i know
is yesterday
in stuttgart
there were clouds
you were one
night's distance
my calendar
each dark hour
widens an age
since you

The Julie Ruin


when the three of them stopped speaking to her

the singer of the band bikini kill, kathleen hanna,

locked herself in her room and produced

a solo album empty of past riots on a machine

that made a padded sound like a heart beat

she called it bedroom art: girls alone in their room

connected to a network of other girls alone

through the blanketing walls she renamed herself

ruin: noun, remnants of something decayed or

destroyed from a former/presumably great state

of being--the hands are still just marble softened

limestone laid was the mistake but no one talks

about how it rained each day for two years, no one

wants to talk about the weather when its cruel

call it a monument to victimhood, why some women

call themselves girls like it's a wish/bone, storming

Eurydice In Hell


        Orpheus:
   Spell home for me.

        Sisyphus:
   A mirror is a bad apology.

        Orpheus:
   Take my wife's legs like a lyre string.

        Sisyphus:
   A song that runs away.

        Orpheus:
   Her belly but a bowl.

        Sisyphus:
   A birth and a feast.

        Orpheus:
   Spell vanish for me.

        Sisyphus:
   Something that won't stop spilling.

        Orpheus:
   Milky.

        Sisyphus:
   A boulder is a spinning plate.

        Orpheus:
   Chipped.

        Sisyphus:
   Was there ever a story that didn't lie like a legend?

        Orpheus:
   Was there ever a legend that wasn't a threat?

        Sisyphus:
   There's no use singing to a wife who wants to be a song.

        Orpheus:
   Show me a god who wasn't born.

        Sisyphus:
   A sailor who won't drown.

        Orpheus:
   Any abdomen that isn't a ball of yarn.

        Sisyphus:
   Spinning.

        Orpheus:
   Unraveling into knit.
